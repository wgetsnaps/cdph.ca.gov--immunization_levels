Public Health Emergencies

For public health emergencies, please dial 9-1-1 or contact your local health department.
If you live in an area without 911 service, contact your local emergency, medical or police service.
If you need to speak with the CDPH duty officer related to a public health emergency, which poses an immediate health risk, please call (916) 328-3605.
